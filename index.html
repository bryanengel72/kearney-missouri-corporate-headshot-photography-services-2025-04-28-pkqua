Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to my headshot session?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that reflects your industry. Avoid busy patterns and bright colors. We'll provide detailed guidelines upon booking.

How long does a session take?

Standard sessions last 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses and outfits. We ensure you're comfortable and confident throughout the process.

When will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your professionally retouched photos within 3-5 business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you provide hair and makeup services?

Yes, we partner with professional stylists and can arrange hair and makeup services for an additional fee. Please request this service when booking.

What is your rescheduling policy?

We understand that plans change. You can reschedule up to 48 hours before your session at no additional cost. Last-minute changes may incur a fee.
